Overview

CourseSelector is an intelligent course planning application designed to help students navigate their academic journey by identifying eligible courses for upcoming semesters based on completed prerequisites.

Navigate Your Degree

See a clear path from your current courses to graduation.

Prerequisites Tracking

Automatically identify which courses you're eligible to take.

Semester Planning

Plan multiple semesters ahead with confidence.

Avoid scheduling mistakes that could delay graduation.
